# Gate -1 Claim Contract

Frozen: 2026-07-18

## Primary question

Can a source-faithful, typed contract recover what heterogeneous latent-
reasoning releases actually construct, execute, optimize, and expose to
control flow, and do contract-level distinctions predict checkpoint-level
intervention effects beyond strong simple diagnostics?

## Candidate contribution

The candidate paper is an empirical methodology and benchmark paper with three
required contributions:

1. a typed contract with honest non-applicability rather than one universal
   latent metric;
2. source-pinned executable conformance tests for four released methods across
   deterministic recurrence, self-distillation, and stochastic latent RL; and
3. an operational validation showing that contract distinctions add predictive
   value beyond entropy, maximum mixture weight, latent depth, input-only
   controls, and generic causal ablations.

The third contribution is not established at Gate -1.

## Excluded claims

- first evidence that latent reasoning uses shortcuts;
- first causal intervention study of Coconut or CODI;
- first benchmark of latent thought representations;
- first finding that continuous reasoning degrades with depth;
- first exact likelihood or policy-gradient treatment for continuous thoughts;
- a generic claim that source mismatch implies lower task accuracy;
- superiority of one method from raw cross-checkpoint accuracy.

## Unit of analysis

The primary unit is a `(method, checkpoint, native task, intervention)` record.
Cross-method accuracy is not comparable because training data, base models,
prompts, and objectives differ. Cross-method analysis uses within-method
standardized effects and contract categories only.

## Gate -1 pass conditions

All conditions are required:

1. four distinct mechanism families are represented;
2. two are stochastic latent-RL methods;
3. all official source repositories are pinned and hash verified;
4. all four public checkpoints have immutable revisions;
5. at least three checkpoints are official releases and at least three are
   already local and hash verified;
6. deterministic methods mark stochastic policy measure as `N/A`;
7. the broad mechanism-audit claim remains explicitly excluded.

## Permanent KILL conditions

Kill LRC-Bench as a competitive AAAI paper if any occurs:

- an exact direct neighbor already provides source-to-runtime conformance for
  deterministic and stochastic latent-RL methods;
- the empirical findings reduce to known shortcut, decodability, or latent-
  depth results;
- contract categories add no held-out value beyond entropy, maximum weight,
  latent length, and no-latent ablation;
- fewer than three method families pass source-native runtime controls;
- results depend on comparing raw accuracy across incompatible checkpoints;
- policy-contract findings occur only in Latent-GRPO and disappear in
  SofT-GRPO or another stochastic family;
- an effect appears only after changing thresholds, prompts, or interventions
  in response to observed results.

## Promotion rule

Gate -1 authorizes only checkpoint acquisition and source-native fixture
replay. GPU checkpoint evaluation remains unauthorized until every fixture
control is deterministic, reconstructable, and source-equivalent.
