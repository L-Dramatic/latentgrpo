# PCMC Checkpoint Gate A0 Result

Decision date: 2026-07-18

## Frozen decision: KILL PCMC at A0

The preregistered general PCMC hypothesis required independent A0 passes on
both Latent-GRPO-Llama-1B and SofT-GRPO-Qwen-1.5B. Latent-GRPO passed strongly;
SofT-GRPO failed the minimum non-closure threshold by roughly five orders of
magnitude. The overall decision is therefore `KILL_PCMC_GATE_A0`. A1, Gate B,
and training are not authorized.

## Source-equivalence audit

The unexpected cross-checkpoint contrast was red-teamed before accepting the
KILL:

- Latent-GRPO actions execute the existing unmodified pinned official sampler
  replay.
- A second replay harness now executes the unmodified pinned SofT
  `sampler.py` with only import/runtime dependencies stubbed.
- On four real SofT checkpoint prompts, the independent official replay and
  the A0 adapter matched token IDs and floating-point weights exactly at every
  frozen seed.
- Unit tests also compare both paths on synthetic logits.

The SofT failure is not explained by prompt formatting, wrong end token,
sampler order, random seed, or cache sharing.

Latent-GRPO forms genuinely distributed ten-token actions, and its arithmetic
embedding frequently changes the next-token law relative to the hard-branch
teacher. SofT's source-native `tau=0.1` Gumbel-softmax is almost always a hard
token in practice, so arithmetic and branch-mixture transitions close
trivially. That makes a universal PCMC correction unnecessary for one of the
two target policy families.

## Scientific interpretation

This result does not show that mixture non-closure never matters. It shows:

1. non-closure is strongly architecture/sampler dependent;
2. the proposed general PCMC method lacks the required cross-family phenomenon;
3. selecting only Latent-GRPO after seeing A0 would violate the preregistration
   and weaken the AAAI claim;
4. a Latent-only causal study would still need A1, strong hard/top-1 controls,
   and a novelty boundary against distribution distillation and Soft Concept
   Mixing. It is retained as a diagnostic asset, not promoted as the next paper.

## Resource decision

- Do not rent an H20 for PCMC.
- Do not run the 8,192 planned A1 continuations.
- Do not implement B0/B1 or train a PCMC adapter.
- Freeze all code, complete records, positive Latent result, negative SofT
  result, and exact decision artifacts for future reuse.

With OMPI-R already killed by exact method identity and PCMC now killed by its
checkpoint gate, the external report's own terminal condition is met: there is
currently no justified training-ready idea in that two-candidate plan. The next
step is a fresh, collision-first idea search rather than weakening either gate.
