# P1 Additive Collision and Claim-Boundary Record

**Date:** 2026-07-16  
**Status:** binding post-P0 design addendum; hash pending final P1 freeze  
**Decision:** `PASS-DESIGN / BLOCK-EXECUTION`  
**Scope:** additive only; the immutable P0 files and hashes are unchanged

## 1. Residual claim that remains ownable

The project does **not** own a new Fisher matrix, path geometry, generic latent
instability result, or parameter-to-activation equivalence. The narrow residual
claim is:

> For source-derived natural updates at an early recursive latent action, short
> observable horizons can make practically consequential false-safe decisions
> at equal acceptance coverage, even after trace/support/exit discontinuities
> are removed; a later method must decide when short evidence is sufficient at
> lower matched cost than long Fisher, direct continuation Monte Carlo, recurrent
> stability, and adapted-metric competitors.

P1 can establish only the phenomenon, specificity, decision consequence, and
method opportunity. A strong AAAI method claim additionally requires a P2
adaptive procedure that does not routinely compute the full long horizon.

| [Weight Updates as Activation Shifts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00425) | general first-order conditions connecting weight updates and activation interventions | P1's behavioral phenomenon remains distinct, but a generic first-order parameter/activation bridge cannot be claimed as new and may serve only as later empirical validation |
| classical path Fisher / natural gradient | finite-horizon trajectory Fisher, local KL, and `F^{-1}g` optimization | these are risk objects and baselines only, never the headline novelty |

No reviewed neighbor currently combines all five binding ingredients:

1. source-generated early recursive latent updates;
2. trace-stable expected continuation risk under an explicitly controlled law;
3. simultaneously confirmed short-horizon false-safe decisions at common
   coverage;
4. same-vector late and ordinary autoregressive specificity controls; and
5. a compute-aware adaptive sufficiency method that beats direct-MC, stability,
   and adapted-metric alternatives.

This is a residual novelty judgment, not proof that no concurrent paper exists.
A fresh collision search is mandatory immediately before submission.

## 3. Binding nonclaims and wording

- Family A is a **source-proposed exploration joint action under deterministic
  downstream latent closure**, not a complete training or deployment trajectory.
- Family B is a **source-objective-derived hidden-state counterfactual**, not an
  optimizer, parameter update, policy-head update, or pathwise answer gradient.
- The full-support visible law is a controlled analysis distribution, not the
  released top-30/top-p sampler.
- `q_H(v)` is a directional expected-history risk object, not a newly invented
  Fisher and not a global certificate.
- The cross-checked adapted-metric score predicts a frozen finite-history label
  statistic; it is not a theorem or upper confidence bound on true expected KL.
- A fixed `H_cert` P1 result is a phenomenon witness, not the final adaptive
  method.
- Two construction families on Latent-GRPO do not establish mechanism
  generality.
- Generic first-order weight-to-activation transport is attributed to prior work
  and cannot be sold as a theoretical contribution.

## 4. Minimum path to an AAAI strong-method paper

The strong path requires all of:

1. both preregistered Latent-GRPO families pass without collapsing under the
   direction-span gate;
2. the early interface beats natural-late, `LATE-MATCHED-V`,
   `AR-MATCHED-V`, and `AR-ACTION` controls;
3. H8/FishBack-style, recurrent stability, adapted metric, and matched-time
   direct-MC each lose by the frozen practical margins;
4. P2 adaptively controls both evidence horizon and history count without first
   evaluating the full long object, and wins on the binding end-to-end wall-time
   frontier;
5. a qualified CODI, SWITCH, or other materially different recurrence reproduces
   the early-specific phenomenon; SofT-GRPO alone is only same-mechanism
   robustness;
6. at least one later family is induced by an actual optimizer/checkpoint delta,
   followed by multi-step training-risk validation; and
7. prompt/reasoning-family and distribution-shift robustness are shown.

Without P2 and a mechanism-distinct replication, the ceiling is a careful
diagnostic paper rather than the intended strong method paper.

## 5. Collision-driven kill conditions

Kill the strong line if any occurs:

- early specificity does not exceed both matched late and ordinary controls;
- FishBack/H8, stability, adapted metric, or direct-MC comes within the frozen
  `0.03` practical-equivalence margin;
- the effect is explained by proxy, stop, exit, support, or trace churn;
- A/B collapse to one effective family and no untouched optimizer family is
  available;
- P2 nearly always computes `H_cert`, merely increases `H` until stable, or is
  dominated on end-to-end cost;
- mechanism-distinct replication fails; or
- actual optimizer/training validation breaks the activation-risk bridge.

The current decision remains `PASS-DESIGN / BLOCK-EXECUTION`: the claim has a
defensible residual space, but no empirical claim is authorized or established.
